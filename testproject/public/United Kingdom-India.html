INDIA-"A vibrant mosaic of cultures, wisdom, and enduring innovation""

Taj Mahal, Agra, Uttar Pradesh

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love and architectural beauty.

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for its stunning palaces, vibrant markets, and historical forts, including the Hawa Mahal and Amber Fort.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

A spiritual and cultural hub on the banks of the Ganges River, Varanasi is renowned for its ancient temples, ghats, and unique blend of tradition and mysticism.

Kerala Backwaters, Kerala

Experience the serene beauty of Kerala by exploring its backwaters on a houseboat. The lush landscapes, tranquil waters, and traditional villages offer a peaceful retreat.

Mahabalipuram, Tamil Nadu

Known for its UNESCO-listed ancient rock-cut temples and stunning Shore Temple, Mahabalipuram in Tamil Nadu is a treasure trove of Dravidian architecture and rich cultural heritage.
