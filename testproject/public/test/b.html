Rio de Janeiro
Rio de Janeiro, often referred to as Rio, is a vibrant city in Brazil known for its beautiful beaches, iconic landmarks like Christ the Redeemer, and the annual Carnival festival. The city offers a rich cultural experience and breathtaking natural scenery.
Fortaleza
Fortaleza is a coastal city in northeastern Brazil known for its lively atmosphere, stunning beaches, and rich cultural heritage. The city is a popular tourist destination, offering a mix of modern attractions and historical sites for visitors to explore.
