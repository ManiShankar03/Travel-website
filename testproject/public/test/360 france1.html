Paris, France

Paris, the capital of France, is a city renowned for its iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. It is a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture, making it one of the most visited cities in the world. The romantic ambiance, charming streets, and rich history contribute to the allure of Paris, creating an unforgettable experience for visitors from around the globe.
