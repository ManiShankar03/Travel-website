Taj Mahal, India

The Taj Mahal, an iconic white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, is a symbol of eternal love. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. The Taj Mahal's exquisite architecture and intricate detailing attract millions of visitors each year, making it a testament to timeless beauty and architectural brilliance.
