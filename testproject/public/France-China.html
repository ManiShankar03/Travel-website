REVEALING THE MYSTERY OF CHINA

The Great Wall of China:

A historical marvel and UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Great Wall is a must-visit for its breathtaking views and rich cultural significance.

The Forbidden City, Beijing

A palace complex that served as the imperial palace for Chinese emperors for centuries, showcasing traditional Chinese architecture and housing a vast collection of artifacts.

The Terracotta Army, Xi'an

A fascinating archaeological site featuring thousands of life-sized terracotta soldiers and horses, guarding the tomb of China's first emperor, Qin Shi Huang.

Li River, Guilin

Known for its stunning karst landscape, the Li River offers picturesque views of limestone peaks, bamboo groves, and traditional fishing villages.

The Yellow Mountains (Huangshan)

Renowned for its granite peaks, ancient pine trees, and hot springs, the Yellow Mountains provide a serene and breathtaking natural experience.

Exciting Activities

Discover and enjoy a variety of activities during your stay.

Yangshuo Bamboo Rafting

Experience the serene beauty of Yangshuo by taking a bamboo raft ride on the Yulong River. Enjoy the picturesque karst landscapes and lush greenery along the way.

Peking Opera Performance

Immerse yourself in traditional Chinese performing arts by attending a Peking Opera show in Beijing. Witness vibrant costumes, elaborate makeup, and a unique blend of music and acrobatics.

Explore Zhangjiajie National Forest Park

Discover the surreal landscapes that inspired the movie "Avatar" by exploring the towering sandstone pillars of Zhangjiajie. Take a cable car ride for panoramic views of the breathtaking scenery.

Calligraphy Class in Xi'an

Embrace Chinese culture by participating in a calligraphy class in Xi'an. Learn the art of writing Chinese characters with brush and ink, gaining insights into the country's rich linguistic heritage.

Hot Air Balloon Ride in Guilin

Get a bird's-eye view of Guilin's stunning karst formations by taking a hot air balloon ride. Drift peacefully above the landscapes, capturing unforgettable views of the unique geological features.

Visit the Giant Panda Research Base

Connect with China's beloved giant pandas at the Giant Panda Research Base in Chengdu. Witness these adorable creatures in their natural habitat and learn about conservation efforts for this endangered species.
